Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Creative Technology LLC doing business as Flomatix ("Flomatix," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of Mini, our project management assistant for Microsoft Teams (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information from Microsoft Teams

When you install and use Mini in Microsoft Teams, we collect the following information through Microsoft's APIs:

  • User Identity: Azure Active Directory Object ID, email address, display name, first name, last name, and tenant ID
  • Activity Data: Last seen timestamp and active status

1.2 Information You Provide

When you use the Service, you may provide:

  • Project Information: Project names, descriptions, tasks, milestones, and RAID items (Risks, Assumptions, Issues, Dependencies)
  • Team Information: Team member assignments and roles
  • Status Updates: Task updates, summaries, and progress information submitted through the bot

1.3 Information Collected Automatically

We automatically collect certain technical information:

  • Audit Information: Timestamps and user IDs for record creation and updates
  • Technical Data: Through Azure Application Insights, we collect performance metrics, error logs, and usage telemetry to maintain and improve the Service

1.4 Information We Do Not Collect

We do not store full conversation transcripts between you and the Mini bot. We only store structured responses (such as status update summaries) that you explicitly submit.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service
  • Send you task update requests and reminders through Microsoft Teams
  • Display project status and team progress in dashboards
  • Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Respond to your requests and support inquiries
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

Third Party Purpose Data Shared
Microsoft (Graph API) User profile lookups Email address to retrieve user details
Microsoft (Teams Bot Framework) Sending proactive messages Task update requests sent to users
Microsoft Azure Infrastructure hosting All Service data (stored in Azure SQL Database)

We may also share information:

  • With your consent or at your direction
  • To comply with legal process or government requests
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Flomatix, our users, or others
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with notice to you)

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on Microsoft Azure infrastructure in the Central US region. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit (TLS/SSL)
  • Secure credential management through Azure Key Vault
  • Access controls and authentication through Azure Active Directory
  • Regular security monitoring through Azure Application Insights

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you the Service. Specifically:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while you actively use the Service
  • Inactive Accounts: If you stop using the Service, we may retain your data for up to 12 months before deletion
  • Deletion Requests: Upon request, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal purposes

6. Your Rights and Choices

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate personal data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data
  • Export: Request a portable copy of your data
  • Objection: Object to certain processing of your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at admin@flomatix.ai.

7. International Data Transfers

Your information is stored and processed in the United States (Azure Central US region). If you are located outside the United States, your information will be transferred to and processed in the United States. By using the Service, you consent to this transfer.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If we learn we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on this page with a new "Last updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
